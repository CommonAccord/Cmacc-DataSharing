This is a collection of "prose objects," codified legal documents, forms and widgets indeed to lead to a generalized framework and set of supporting materials for data sharing - initially focused on sharing of information relating to epidemics. You can use these prose objects privately by creating an "instance" of CommonAccord - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1-06FzulLpQ.
Transacting platforms can integrate them by writing their own rendering engine. Let us hear from you. commonaccord@gmail.com
